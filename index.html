Nadiia Mori Calderon

At Mori Calderon Talent Management, we believe that entertainment is the heartbeat of the cruise experience. Founded by Nadiia Mori Calderon, our boutique agency specializes in scouting and representing high-caliber musicians, vocalists, and specialty acts specifically for the maritime industry. With a deep understanding of the unique demands of life at sea, we don’t just find jobs—we build careers. We take pride in our meticulous selection process, ensuring that every artist in our roster possesses not only world-class technical skill but also the charisma and professionalism required by the world’s leading cruise lines. Our mission is simple: to provide cruise directors with seamless talent solutions and to empower artists to share their craft across the world’s oceans. At Mori Calderon, your talent is our management’s masterpiece.
